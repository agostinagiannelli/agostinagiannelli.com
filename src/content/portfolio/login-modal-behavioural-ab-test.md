subtitle: "Behavioural copy testing to increase login interactions"
category: "Hospitality & Travel"
tools: [Adobe Target, GA4, Quantum Metric]
overview: "Experiment tested behavioural copy variations in a login modal offering a discount incentive."
challenge: "Many users closed the login modal without interacting with the offer."
hypothesis: "Behavioural copy on the dismiss action could increase attention and modal interaction."
implementation: "Tested different copy variations for the modal close CTA, including behavioural framing (“No, I don’t want the discount”)."
outcome: "Increased interaction with the modal and improved login conversions."
